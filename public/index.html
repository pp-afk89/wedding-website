Our Story

Rakel and Piers met for the first time in Hackney, September 2022. They shared a bottle of Cava and made their way through a bougie tapas menu, before having a couple of Guinness at the Auld Shillelagh. It all went well, despite Rakel only having one working ear thanks to a popped eardrum (it definitely wasn't COVID, guys). Maybe that helped Piers' case. After more than 3 years together, their Hackney life hasn't changed much.

They were engaged in July 2025 in Paris, the City of Love. Amidst a dreary few days, the clouds parted and the sun shone on Parc des Buttes-Chaumont for the (young) couple as Piers asked Rakel to spend the rest of her life with him. She said yes, although he had to re-confirm, to be sure his ears were working this time. They spent the rest of the day excitedly messaging all of you.

The couple's wedding guests come from all over the world: Turkey, the UK, the US, Israel, Puerto Rico, and from across the continent. Close friends and family who have played important parts in each of their lives so far. In summer 2026, they'll be moving to Boston in the US, where a new chapter in their shared life begins. (Don't worry, Frazer's coming too!)
